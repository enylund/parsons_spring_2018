  • Non-Computer Type

  • Bring in your first sketches and ideas for this mini-project.


    The format is:

  • — Portrait Orientation
  • — Swiss Poster Size (90.5 x 128 cm (approximately 35.5 x 50.5 inches))
  • — 3 colors* and each color has to take up at least 25% of the space of the canvas.
  • — handmade type, be creative. (Found objects, torn paper, dripped paint, food, photos, painted tiles, photos of type viewed through a distorting medium, washed paper**, whatever) Just make sure that everything is made up out of the chosen 3 colors
  • — all type should be at least 1 inch tall
  • — your own message (write something yourself. Whether it’s a birthday message, a wish, part of a manifesto, what the content is doesn’t matter. Just make sure it’s something you think is worth saying in some way)
  • — the final deliverable is a pdf

  • *the background color is also a color so it’s not white + 3 colors
  • **if using photographs of type have it be found type
